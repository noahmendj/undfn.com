About

Finding the quiet moment inside the frame.

I'm a documentary and portrait photographer with over a decade of experience working across editorial, commercial, and fine-art contexts. My work focuses on the overlooked — a glance between strangers, the light inside a late-night diner, the stillness that arrives just after something changes.

My photographs have appeared in The New York Times Magazine, TIME, and Le Monde, and have been exhibited in Los Angeles, New York, and Berlin. I studied at the Art Center College of Design in Pasadena and have been documenting the American Southwest since 2015.

I'm currently accepting commissions for editorial portraits and long-form documentary projects.

About

Undefined Photography

I am a photographer based in the Bay Area. Undfn is short for undefined, as I choose not to define myself as a singular type of photographer.

I currently work as a photojournalist for the Daily Californian and a photographer for FAST at Cal. I have and will continue to complete independant portrait, event, and street photography projects.

Available for any style of photo commissions.
